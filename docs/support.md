## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
**Q: How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?**  
A: You can submit a request via GitHub Issues or email support@penss.app.

**Q: Is Penss free to use?**  
A: Yes, Penss is open-source and free for personal and commercial use.

**Q: How do I access the App Store version?**  
A: Search for "Penss" on the App Store or visit [apps.apple.com/app/penss](https://apps.apple.com/app/penss).
